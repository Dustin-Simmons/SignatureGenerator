Gmail Instructions

  • Open Gmail
  • In the top right, click the 'Settings' drop-down button. Click 'See all settings'.
  • In the 'General' tab, scroll down until you see the 'Signature' section.
  • Click the '+ Create New' button. Enter the name for your signature.
  • In the Signature Generator, ensure you have highlighted the entirety of your newly created signature. Then, copy/paste it into the Signature text box in Gmail.
  • At the bottom of the page, click 'Save Changes'.

Apple Mail Instructions

  • Unfortunately Apple Mail does not always format the signature properly if it is copy/pasted from Safari. If these steps do not work, you will have to open the Signature Generator in Google Chrome.
  • Open the Mail App
  • Select the 'Mail' dropdown in the top left. Then select 'Preferences'.
  • In the popup window, select the 'Signatures' tab. Click the '+'.
  • DESELECT the 'Always match my default message font' box.
  • In the Signature Generator, ensure you have highlighted the entirety of your newly created signature.
  • Copy & Paste your signature into the text box.
  • The Simply Mac logo will appear as a blank box so compose a new email to confirm signature is correct.
